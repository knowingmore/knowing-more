02 — Brand Direction

Where we're going.

Four words that define every design decision. When in doubt, ask: does this choice serve all four?

Prestigious Commands attention without shouting. Quiet confidence in every detail.
Luxurious Sparse, unhurried. White space costs nothing but signals everything.
Clinical Ingredient transparency. Formula numbers. Precision over decoration.
Collectible A numbered series, like a perfume house. 001 / 002 / 003.

IM8

im8.com

  • Dark, near-black container — the product doesn't need to shout
  • Gold / metallic typography gives a premium, almost jewellery-like feel
  • Product name is the hero — huge, dominant, central
  • Brand mark is small and discreet above the name
  • Extremely minimal text — every word earns its place
Dark background Gold accents Minimal text Large product name

Simple Nootropics

simplenootropics.com.au

  • Clean, scientific aesthetic — trusted by people who read the research
  • Elegant serif + mono type pairing creates authority
  • Formula / compound name leads — the hero is the product, not the brand
  • Ingredient callouts in small mono text signal transparency
  • No noise — no taglines, no lifestyle copy on the label itself
Clean / minimal Serif + mono Ingredient callouts Scientific authority

05 — Canva Step-by-Step

How to build this in Canva.

Follow these steps in order for each of the three products. Repeat with the correct colours per product.

1

Create the canvas

New design → Custom size → 1200 × 1800 px (portrait). This is a label wrap for a standard supplement canister. Set background to the product colour (#1B2A4A / #A0784A / #C4682A).

2

Add the lid area

Draw a rectangle at the top, full width × 240px tall. Fill with the darker lid colour. Add a thin drop shadow beneath it (offset Y: 10px, blur: 20px, opacity: 30%). This simulates the lid-to-body join.

3

Formula number — top element

Text: "Formula 001" — Inter Medium, 28px, letter-spacing +40, UPPERCASE. Colour: white, 32% opacity. Centre-align, position 60px below the lid edge.

4

Amber rule

Draw a horizontal line: 120px wide × 2px tall. Colour: #E8920A, opacity 65%. Centre it below the formula number, 20px gap.

5

Brand mark

Text: "knowing more." — Playfair Display Regular, 36px, letter-spacing +18, lowercase. Colour: white, 45% opacity. Centre it 16px below the amber rule.

6

Product name — the hero

Text: "Performance" (or Balance / Gut Health) — Playfair Display Medium, 160–180px, letter-spacing −5. White 100%. Then type a separate "." in #E8920A immediately after, same size. Centre both. This is the biggest, most dominant element on the label.

7

Pillar tagline

Text: "Energy · Recovery · Focus" — Inter Light, 24px, letter-spacing +28, UPPERCASE. White, 28% opacity. Centre, 20px below product name.

8

Mid divider line

Full-width horizontal line, 1px, white 10% opacity. This separates the name zone from the ingredient zone. Place it centred vertically on the label.

9

Ingredient callout

Line 1: "NMN · CoQ10 · Alpha-GPC" — Inter SemiBold, 20px, letter-spacing +30, UPPERCASE, white 52% opacity.
Line 2: "Clinically dosed · Full label" — Inter Regular, same size, white 26% opacity.
Both centred, 40px below the mid divider.

10

Certifications strip

Text: "GMP · 3rd Party Tested · Vegan" — Inter Medium, 18px, letter-spacing +22, UPPERCASE. White, 20% opacity. Anchor to 80px from the bottom edge.

11

Serving count

Text: "60 Capsules · 30 Servings" — Inter Regular, 18px, letter-spacing +15, UPPERCASE. White, 22% opacity. Place 20px below certs strip, at the very bottom.

12

Specular highlight (optional but recommended)

Draw a tall narrow rectangle: 60px wide, from lid bottom to base. Left-align it (approx. 80px from left edge). Fill: white, use a left-to-right gradient fade from 8% to 0% opacity. This simulates the cylindrical light catch — immediately makes the can look more 3D and premium.

13

Export & apply to can mockup

Export label as PNG, transparent background. Use a Canva canister mockup (search "supplement bottle mockup" in Canva elements) or upload to Smartmockups / Placeit to wrap the label onto the 3D can. Save as performance.png, balance.png, gut-health.png and replace in /public/images/products/.
