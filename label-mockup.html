Key changes from current design: product name is now the hero (large, central serif), the brand "knowing more." becomes a quiet mark above it. Formula numbers (001–003) add collectible, luxury series identity. Three-line ingredient preview signals clinical credibility. A clean cert strip at the base replaces loose text. The tagline is removed from the label — white space creates the premium feel.

What to remove vs current design

Remove "Good things come to those who are" — the tagline on the label clutters the premium feel. Move it to marketing, not the can.
Remove The large "knowing" wordmark as the visual hero — demote it to a small quiet mark above the product name.
Add Formula series number (001 / 002 / 003) — creates a luxury collectible series feel, like a perfume house.
Add 3 key ingredient callouts in small text — signals clinical transparency without crowding.
Add Subtle drop shadow on the whole can image when placed on white — gives depth and cost.
